EcoStep

Undoubtedly environmental problems are extremely alarming in modern society. So I have decided to make an app “EcoStep” that will help to change this situation.

When I decided to make EcoStep I didn’t know anything about mobile development. So, I decided to take an online course where I would learn Swift. It took me 2 months to complete this course and then I started creating my own first app. I faced a lot of issues and annoying bugs but after 5-6 months of hard work my app was ready for deployment. Now you can find it in the AppStore.
Let me tell you about how this app works. The user should enter his action daily. It can be made by using the action page. There users can find dozens of different actions from eating an apple to taking a bath. For example, the user chooses a food category, then he needs to choose a specific meal and enter the approximate weight of this meal. Then app counts the amount of CO2 which was emitted by the action. The user can see his footprint on the main screen. If the user does not exceed the norm circle will be green. If the user is close to exceeding the norm circle will be yellow. And in case of exceeding the norm circle will be red. The next day CO2 counter will be nullified and the app will give user 3-5 personal tips on how to reduce footprint. At first, users can get only tips about food. These tips are based on actions done the day before. Also, I decided to make a function that is called "Treeks". Users can gain one Treek after entering daily actions. For 3/5/9 Treeks users can open a magic chest that contains drinks cards, transport cards, and housing cards. After gaining 15 cards of one type users gets the ability to get tips not only for food but also for drinks, transportation, or housing. So that’s how EcoStep works.

Also we have an instagram account where we tell our subscribers about enviroment.
