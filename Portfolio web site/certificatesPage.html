Sports

My first hobby is sports

I like it for many reasons, firstly, sport gives me amazing emotions. Secondly, while I am doing any kind of sport I forget about all my problems. What’s more, after a training session I feel relaxed and confident.

Probably, you have already understood that my favourite sport is tennis. I have been playing tennis since I was 4. This sport plays an enormous role in my life, it influenced me in different ways, thanks to tennis now I know that I should never give up, that hard work pays off and also tennis made me feel way more self-confident. I can’t even imagine my life without it.

I also really love to watch professional tennis championships. I have attended them as a viewer approximately 5 times.
Also, I have a collection of tennis balls signed by professional players and even a photo with Andrey Rublev. At the moment he is one of the best tennis players worldwide.

By the way, I also do some extreme sports, as they give me an enormous portion of adrenaline. My favourite extreme sport is snowboarding, I have been doing it for 6 years already. I prefer to snowboard with my friends as it is great fun. Even though sometimes it can be scary or even dangerous I am not planning to give up this activity, as I got amazing emotions from it.

Technologies

My second hobby is technologies and everything related to them


My second hobby is technologies and everything related to them. From a really young age, I was interested in Lego Technics. All-day long I was building different models, it was so impressive and breathtaking, the most amazing part of this process was making these models move by using small electric engines. Moreover, I attended some robotics courses when I was 10 years old. There I was told how to made small and simple programms for robots. This would allow them to move or sort different colored balls

I think these are the main reasons for my interest in technologies nowadays.
Usually, I can spend hours watching different videos on YouTube, which explain how robotics or computers work, also I visit a lot of technological exhibitions, they are very fascinating for me, as I can interact with robots’ software and try to build something by myself.
